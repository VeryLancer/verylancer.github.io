About Game

Dive deep into the surreal landscapes of the mind in Mindscape, a 2D puzzle platformer where every stage embodies a different emotion. Fear, joy, sadness, anxiety — each feeling shapes the world around you, twisting visuals, mechanics, and atmosphere into an immersive emotional journey.

Designed as a psychological and atmospheric adventure, Mindscape invites you to not just play, but feel your way through its ever-shifting dreamscapes. Solve puzzles, adapt to unique challenges, and discover the hidden story stitched between emotions.
